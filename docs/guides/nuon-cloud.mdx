Nuon Cloud is the fully managed, multi-tenant version of Nuon hosted by Nuon. There is no infrastructure to provision or operate — create an account, configure your app, and start deploying software to your customers.

To get started, [sign up at app.nuon.co](https://app.nuon.co) or [contact sales](https://nuon.co/contact-sales).

## What's Included

- Fully managed control plane — no EKS, RDS, Temporal, or ClickHouse to operate
- Automatic upgrades as Nuon releases new versions
- Shared infrastructure with logical tenant isolation
- Full access to the Nuon dashboard, CLI, and API

## Next Steps

Once your account is created, head to [Getting Started](/get-started/quickstart) to connect your first app.
